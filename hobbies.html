Hobbies & Interests

Art has been a lifelong passion of mine — over the years, I've competed in multiple art competitions and continue to explore different styles and mediums, with gouache being my current favorite.

Feel free to check out my art instagram: @sahanacreates
I have been training in Bharathanatyam (Indian Classical Dance) since I was 6 years old, and have performed at multiple venues in the Bay Area. I performed my arangetram (solo debut) in June 2023, and continue to pursue my passion for dance.
